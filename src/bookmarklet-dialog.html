A button on your browser's bookmark bar will allow you to easily access match graphs from the official League of Legends Match History page. Follow these steps to add the bookmarklet:

1. Click and drag the Creep Stats button (below) to your browser's bookmark bar.
 2. Visit the League of Legends Match History page and log in.
 3. Click on one of your matches to view its detail.
 4. Click the Creep Stats bookmark on your bookmark bar.
